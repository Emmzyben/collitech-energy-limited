CUSTOMIZED SOLAR SYSTEM DESIGN

Connect with us today to experience all round power supply.

Maintenance and monitoring services

Is your home powered by the sun? Solar panels are a fantastic way to generate clean energy, but even the best systems need a little more Preventive Maintenance. That’s why at Collitech, we offer comprehensive maintenance and monitoring services to ensure your solar system operates at peak performance for years to come.

Here's why you should choose us:

  • Preventative Care: Our scheduled maintenance helps identify and address minor issues before they become major problems. This extends the lifespan of your solar equipment and saves you money in the long run.
  • Real-Time Monitoring: We use advanced monitoring systems to track your system's performance 24/7. This allows us to detect any potential problems remotely and take swift action.
  • Expert Technicians: Our certified technicians are highly trained in solar system maintenance and repairs. They'll quickly diagnose any issues and get your system back online.
  • Peace of Mind: Relax and enjoy the benefits of solar power knowing your system is in good hands. We take care of everything, so you don't have to worry.

BATTERY SYSTEMS

Most homes rely on solar energy for running of their day-to-day activities, and a reliable battery system is essential for storing solar power and keeping your lights on, even during outages. This is why we at Collitech, we're your one-stop resource centre for all your battery needs!

We offer a complete range of battery system services:

  • Expert Installation: Our certified technicians will install a high-quality battery system seamlessly into your existing solar setup, ensuring optimal performance and safety.
  • Preventative Maintenance: Regular maintenance keeps your batteries operating at peak capacity and extends their lifespan. We'll clean connections, check health, and perform software updates.
  • Prompt Repairs: Power outages can happen unexpectedly. Our team is available to diagnose and repair any battery issues quickly and efficiently, minimizing downtime.
